What is ILO?

ILO is a cross platform, open source, and free piece of software designed to make running the technical back end of theatre as easy as possible. ILO is for anyone who wants to control theatrical technology but doesn't want to deal with the hassle or cost of traditional control systems.

ILO was created in 2016 by Akiva Krauthamer as his senior thesis project at Carnegie Mellon University.

Features

All the features you want, none of the ones you don't.

Lights

Control theatre lights over DMX. Traditional lighting control can get pretty complex, ILO keeps it as simple as possible while still giving you the power to get the job done.

Sounds

Play sound and music cues with ease. Simply add sound files from your computer to the cue stack to have them play back during the show.

Videos

ILO makes it simple to incorporate video and media in to live performance. Just add image or video files and see them on a second display be it a TV screen or a projector.

Open Sound Control

OSC is a powerful networking tool used to trigger events and send messages for live performance. With OSC any other show control system can be connected to ILO with ease.
